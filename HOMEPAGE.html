Welcome to PC Card Wiki!

This page provides information on PC cards using different visuals. Generally, PC cards are removable devices that are plugged into a computer to add memory or input/output capabilities. They are about the size of a credit card. The Gallery section contains labeled images of these cards. Videos on these cards may found in Video Links page whose link you may find above.
